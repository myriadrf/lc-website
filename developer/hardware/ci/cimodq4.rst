CIMODQ4
=======

4x LTE modem bank with VoLTE support.

Theory of operation
-------------------

.. figure:: /images/

Four `Quectel Mini PCIe EVB`_ boards provide 4x `Quectel EC25`_ Mini PCIe modems connected via a USB hub to a front-panel USB port.
